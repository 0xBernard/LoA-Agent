# Service Providers

Service Providers are professional entities engaged by the Aave DAO to perform specific operational, risk, and growth tasks. They are compensated through governance proposals (ARFC/AIP).

## Active Service Providers (2025-2026)

Based on recent governance reforms and budget cycles:

### Aave Chan Initiative (ACI)
*   **Role:** Governance facilitation, growth, and operational execution.
*   **Key Responsibilities:**
    *   Publication of Skywards TEMP CHECK business cases.
    *   Drafting and publishing ARFCs and Snapshots.
    *   Delivering draft AIP payloads for review within deadlines.
    *   General governance coordination.

### TokenLogic
*   **Role:** Financial management and treasury optimization.
*   **Key Responsibilities:**
    *   Execution of monthly buyback management.
    *   Submission of Finance AIP payloads on time each month.
    *   Strategic treasury diversification and yield generation.

### Chaos Labs
*   **Role:** Risk Management.
*   **Key Responsibilities:**
    *   Conducting ARFC risk reviews.
    *   Providing parameter recommendations (Supply Caps, Borrow Caps, Interest Rates).
    *   Maintaining risk monitoring dashboards.
    *   "Risk Steward" automated updates for caps and rates.

### LlamaRisk
*   **Role:** Risk Management (often focusing on specific asset classes or second opinions).
*   **Key Responsibilities:**
    *   ARFC risk reviews aligned with publication deadlines.
    *   Assessment of new asset listings and collateral types.

## Service Provider Compensation Reform (V4 Alignment)

As Aave transitions to V4, the DAO is reforming compensation to align incentives with the new architecture. Key goals include:
*   **Performance-based Pay:** Linking compensation to specific deliverables (e.g., AIP payloads, timely reviews).
*   **Long-term Vesting:** Ensuring providers are aligned with the protocol's long-term health.
*   **V4 Readiness:** Ensuring providers are equipped to support the new "Hub-and-Spoke" model.
