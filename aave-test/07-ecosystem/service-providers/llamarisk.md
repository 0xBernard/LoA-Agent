# Service Provider: LlamaRisk

**Role:** Qualitative Risk Analysis & Asset Onboarding
**Handle:** `@LlamaRisk`
**Focus:** "Long-Tail" Assets, RWA (Real World Assets), and Second Opinions.

## Overview
LlamaRisk provides a specialized layer of risk assessment that complements the quantitative work of Chaos Labs. While Chaos uses simulations, LlamaRisk often performs **Qualitative Audits**—looking at the legal structure of assets, the reliability of their teams, and the decentralization of their oracles.

## Key Responsibilities
*   **Asset Due Diligence:** Performing deep-dives into new collateral types (e.g., eETH, sDAI, or RWA tokens) before they are listed.
*   **Oracle Assessments:** Reviewing whether a Chainlink feed or other oracle is sufficient for a specific asset's liquidity.
*   **Second Opinions:** Providing an independent check on Chaos Labs' parameter recommendations to ensure a "Defense-in-Depth" approach to risk.

## Methodology
LlamaRisk reports are often characterized by:
1.  **Transparency:** Detailed breakdowns of an asset's smart contracts.
2.  **Risk Frameworks:** Using standardized scores for "Liquidity Risk," "Volatility Risk," and "Smart Contract Risk."
3.  **Holistic View:** Considering how an asset on Aave interacts with the broader DeFi ecosystem (e.g., its presence on Curve or Uniswap).

## Notable Work
*   **LST Analysis:** Extensive reporting on Liquid Staking Tokens (like `wstETH`, `cbETH`) and their de-pegging risks.
*   **Isolation Mode Recommendations:** Helping the DAO decide which new assets should be restricted to "Isolation Mode" versus "Core" collateral.
