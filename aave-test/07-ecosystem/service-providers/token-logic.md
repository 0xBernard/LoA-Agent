# Service Provider: TokenLogic

**Role:** Financial Strategy & GHO Growth
**Handle:** `@TokenLogic`
**Focus:** Treasury Management, GHO Peg, and DAO Revenue.

## Overview
TokenLogic is a financial-centric Service Provider that acts as the "CFO" or "Treasury Manager" for the Aave DAO. Their primary focus is ensuring the DAO's assets are productive, the GHO stablecoin maintains its peg, and the protocol generates sustainable revenue.

## Key Responsibilities
*   **Treasury Management:** Managing the "Aave Collector" (the wallet that collects protocol fees), swapping assets for AAVE to build DAO voting power, or diversifying into stablecoins.
*   **GHO Stability:** Monitoring the GHO peg and proposing "GHO Stability Modules" (GSM) or interest rate changes to keep GHO at $1.00.
*   **Buyback Programs:** Executing the DAO's mandate to buy back AAVE from the market using protocol revenue.
*   **Financial Reporting:** Providing the DAO with transparent updates on revenue, expenses, and treasury health.

## Notable Projects
*   **GHO Growth:** Leading the "GHO Growth Service Provider" initiative to increase adoption across Arbitrum and other L2s.
*   **Safety Module (SM) Reform:** Proposing changes to how AAVE stakers are rewarded and how the Safety Module should act as a "Backstop" for the protocol.

## Relationship with other SPs
*   **With Chaos Labs:** TokenLogic works with Chaos to set the interest rates for GHO. Chaos provides the risk analysis, while TokenLogic provides the financial strategy for growth.
*   **With ACI:** They often co-author proposals for onboarding GHO to new DEXs (like Curve or Maverick).
