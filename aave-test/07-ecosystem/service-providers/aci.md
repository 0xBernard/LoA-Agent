## Overview
The Aave Chan Initiative (ACI) is a delegate platform and service provider dedicated to the growth and effective governance of the Aave protocol. Unlike risk providers who focus on "Defense," ACI often focuses on "Offense" (Growth, Business Development, Strategic Partnerships).

## Key Responsibilities
*   **Skywards Program:** Facilitating the onboarding of new assets via "Skywards" (Aave's launchpad-like mechanism).
*   **Governance Coordination:** Drafting AIPs (Aave Improvement Proposals) and ensuring they move smoothly through the pipeline (Temp Check -> ARFC -> AIP).
*   **Orbit Program:** A delegate platform initiative to onboard and incentivize diverse voices in the DAO.
*   **Protocol Upgrades:** ACI is often the driving force behind "Direct-to-AIP" technical upgrades (e.g., v3.1, v3.2 deployments).

## Philosophy
ACI advocates for an "Aave First" approach, prioritizing revenue generation for the DAO and aggressive expansion to new chains (e.g., Scroll, Sonic, ZKsync). They are strong proponents of the "GHO" stablecoin and often propose strategies to maintain its peg and adoption.
