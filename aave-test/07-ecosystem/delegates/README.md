## Delegate Platforms
*   **Orbit:** A DAO-funded initiative to professionalize delegation. It provides a platform for newer delegates to gain voting power and get compensated for their governance work.

## Service Provider Ecosystem
*   **Risk:** Chaos Labs, LlamaRisk.
*   **Finance:** TokenLogic.
*   **Dev / Tech:** BGD Labs (Bored Ghosts Developing).
*   **Growth / Ops:** ACI.

## Key Trends (2025-2026)
1.  **Specialization:** The DAO has moved away from generalist grants to specific "Service Provider" contracts with strict KPIs.
2.  **Risk Diversification:** Using multiple risk providers (Chaos + LlamaRisk) to avoid single points of failure in judgment.
3.  **Treasury Maturity:** TokenLogic's emergence highlights a shift towards treating the DAO treasury as an active investment fund rather than a passive wallet.
