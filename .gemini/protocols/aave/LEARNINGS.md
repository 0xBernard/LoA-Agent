## Governance Quirks

- **"Aave Alignment"**: A central theme in 2024+ governance. This refers to the DAO's strategy of rewarding loyal participants and penalizing those who support competitors (specifically Morpho).
- **Merit System**: The technical implementation of "Alignment." Users who borrow GHO or provide liquidity on Aave are rewarded; users who migrate to Morpho have their Merit rewards slashed.
- **GHO sensitivity**: Any GHO-related discussion attracts high engagement and strong opinions.
- **Parameter fatigue**: Community shows declining engagement on routine LTV/cap adjustments.
- **V3 migration discussions**: Often contentious, involve multiple stakeholder groups.
